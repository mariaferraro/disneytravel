Pandora's Reefs

Metkayina Clan

Visit the Metkayina Clan along the shores of the Pandoran oceans. Sleep in a Marui pod built into the roots of mangrove-like trees spanning the islands. Discover the islands and the waters surrounding, home to many unique specimens of fauna and flora, such as the dorado verde, pincer fish, and papa mantis tree. See the Cove of the Ancestors, an area sacred to the Metkayina and swim with tulkuns, the whale-like creatures of Pandora.
